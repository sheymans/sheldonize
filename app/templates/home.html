What is this?

Sheldonize is a task manager that automatically schedules your to-dos on your calendar. Traditional task managers are just lists. They require you to do all the hard work of manually planning your to-dos yourself. However, with Sheldonize that planning happens automatically: Sheldonize checks your existing meetings, and plans your work around them, while making sure deadlines for your tasks are met. The planning is completely flexible and happens with the click of a button, such that when meetings get shifted or more important stuff comes in, Sheldonize can quickly reschedule your work.

If you currently keep your meetings in Google Calendar, then you can continue doing so: Sheldonize pulls in those calendar events and schedules around them.

If you like the sound of that, check out our 3-minute overview video on our sister blog, I Woke Up This Morning.
