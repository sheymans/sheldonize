Privacy Policy

Your and your data's privacy is important to us. We will only access your account to help you with problems, to fix software bugs, or to satisfy legal requirements.

  1. General Policy. When you sign up for Sheldonize, we asked only for your email address. We use this address to send you updates or essential information. We will never selll your personal information or content to third parties, and we will not use your name for marketing purposes without your permission.

    When you are in contact with Sheldonize with questions, we will keep that correspondence and email address for future reference.

    If Sheldonize is acquired by or merged with another company, we will notify you well before any information about you is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

  2. Trial period. Sheldonize does not ask the user's payment information at sign-up for the trial. Only when the user would decide to sign up for a paying account, Sheldonize will ask the user's payment information.

  3. Law enforcement. Sheldonize will hand over your data when legally required to do so. Unless we are legally prevented from doing so, we will always inform you when such requests are made.

  4. Encryption. All data is encrypted via SSL/TLS when transmitted from our servers to your browser.

  5. Security of Payment. All payment transactions are handled by stripe, a secure way of handling online payments. Sheldonize does not store your payment information.

  6. Questions. Sheldonize may update this policy. You can access, change or delete your personal information at any time by contacting support@sheldonize.com; you can also ask any other questions regarding Privacy on that address.
