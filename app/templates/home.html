What is this?

Sheldonize is a free task manager that automatically schedules your to-dos on your calendar. Traditional task managers are just lists. They require you to do all the hard work of manually planning your to-dos yourself. However, with Sheldonize that planning happens automatically: Sheldonize checks your existing meetings, and plans your work around them, while making sure deadlines for your tasks are met. The planning is completely flexible and happens with the click of a button, such that when meetings get shifted or more important stuff comes in, Sheldonize can quickly reschedule your work.

Some questions you may have:

  • I'm already using Google Calendar, and I don't want to change

    You should not! We love Google calendar as well. You can simply keep using Google Calendar, sync Sheldonize with it, and then see all your Google Calendar events on the Sheldonize Schedule. We can then schedule around those events.

  • I'm often on the way, how can I quickly add tasks?

    You can simply send an email to hey@sheldonize.com with in the subject your to-do. We'll automatically put that in your Sheldonize Inbox.

  • I'm already using Wunderlist!

    Great! It shows that you take managing your time seriously. Consider this however: does Wunderlist show you your schedule, or is it just a list of to-dos that often overwhelms you? If yes, give Sheldonize a try. We schedule your to-dos.

  • I'd like to keep notes with to-dos and meetings

    You can do that. We even support Markdown.

  • I follow the GTD approach; I love it and don't want to switch.

    You'll be happy to find concepts like the Inbox, the Someday/Maybe view also with us. And we're adding features continuously.

  • And much, much more. See below for a summary of the current features.

Don't forget to check out our 3-minute overview video on our sister blog, I Woke Up This Morning. It will show you Sheldonize in action.
