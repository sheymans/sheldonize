Sheldonize Schedules your Tasks




What is this?

Sheldonize is a task manager that calculates your daily and weekly schedule to work on your tasks, attend your meetings and keep your deadlines. If you currently keep your meetings in Google Calendar, then you can continue doing so: Sheldonize pulls in those calendar events and schedules around them.

If you like the sound of that, check out our 3-minute overview video on our sister blog, I Woke Up This Morning.
